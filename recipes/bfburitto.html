5 Minutes Breakfast Burrito

A delicious quick 5 minutes homemade burrito for your morning pleasure.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons salsa
  • 1 slice reduced-fat American cheese
  • 1 tortilla

Steps

  1. Spray a cereal bowl with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Crack the eggs into the bowl, add the salsa, and stir.
  3. Microwave on high for 1 minute, stir, and cook for another minute or until the mixture firms up.
